One click. Lid closed. Still awake.
The caffeine toggle that actually prevents clamshell sleep.
Because Amphetamine keeps failing.

Download for Mac

Free & open source  ·  v1.0  ·  <1 MB

💊

Actually works

Holds PreventSystemSleep — the assertion that blocks lid-close sleep, not just idle sleep.

⚡

One click

Click the menu bar icon to toggle. Green bolt = caffeinated. Slash = sleeping. Shows how long it's been active.

🚀

Launch at login

Enable once and forget about it. Remembers your last state across restarts.

🪶

Tiny & native

Pure Swift, IOKit directly. No subprocess, no shell commands, no background agents. Under 1 MB.

Getting started

  1. Open the DMG and drag AmphetamineXL to Applications
  2. Open Terminal and run: xattr -cr /Applications/AmphetamineXL.app
  3. Open AmphetamineXL from Applications
  4. Click the ⚡ icon in your menu bar to enable caffeine
  5. Close your lid — your Mac stays awake
